About Sidu

Sidu - Education Management Application, is a School & College Management Software of Palmdec Foundation which assists school administrators, teachers, academic staff, students and parents. It delivers a user-friendly & technologically advanced solution to integrating the daily activities of school and colleges.

As an Tech & Education oriented NGO in Ghana, we have an excellent depth of expertise in taking an idea and turning it into a totally finished product across all related services, from websites to web applications. Our creativity, state of the art understanding and energy can make sure that your future will be successful through our IT Services.
